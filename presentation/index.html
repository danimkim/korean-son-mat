Korean Recipe Discovery

Cook Korean with what’s already in your kitchen — find recipes by the ingredients you have, filter by diet, difficulty, and time.

🤖 Built end-to-end with Claude Code
The idea

“What can I cook tonight?”

Like Supercook, but exclusively Korean cuisine. You tell it what’s in your fridge; it surfaces the dishes you can actually make — ranked by how much you already have.

  • Ingredient-first discovery — type or tap what you have, get ranked matches
  • Filter to your needs — dietary restrictions, difficulty, and cook time
  • Cook with confidence — see what’s missing and how to substitute it
  • 15 curated Korean recipes seeded and ready out of the box

Not just code generation

Claude verified & debugged

While preparing this very demo, Claude found and fixed two real bugs by inspecting the running app — not just writing code, but checking that it behaved.

  • Pantry persistence clobber — the empty first render overwrote saved ingredients on load. Fixed with a hydration gate.
  • Search request race — a slow earlier request overwrote newer results (recipes showed 0 matches). Fixed with a request-id guard.
  • Dev-server 404s — diagnosed a clobbered .next cache and restored it.
  • Kept README.md and requirement.md in sync with reality at every step.
